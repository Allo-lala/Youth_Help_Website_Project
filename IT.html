CCTV systems have evolved significantly in recent years. One of the new modern spy cameras is the IP camera, which uses the internet to transmit images and videos in real-time. Additionally, there are now CCTV systems that use AI technology to enhance the detection of potential threats or unusual behavior.

The uses of CCTV are diverse and widely employed in various settings. For instance CCTV is used in commercial establishments such as shopping centers, banks and offices to enhance productivity, security and deter theft. It is also used in public places such as airports, train stations, and bus terminals to monitor and ensure public safety. In residential settings, CCTV can provide a sense of security for homeowners by monitoring the perimeter of their property and alerting them to any unusual activity.
